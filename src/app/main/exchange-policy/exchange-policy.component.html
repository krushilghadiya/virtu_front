Exchange & Buyback

Policy

Presenting the Virtue Star Lifetime Exchange & Buy-Back Guarantee! This comprehensive policy is extended to all purchases* from Virtue Star, ensuring utmost satisfaction. Customers have the option to return their product, accompanied by the original product certificate, for buy-back or exchange, valuated at its prevailing market value. Deductions will be applied for making charges, taxes, and a nominal processing fee of Rs. 1,500. Moreover, any discounts or complimentary gifts received will be factored into the transaction accordingly

Under the Lifetime Exchange option, the credited value, after deductions, will be added to your Virtue Star account, which can be used towards purchasing any other product. Please note, this value cannot be redeemed for cash. If you opt for Lifetime Buy-Back, the buy-back value will be transferred to you via online bank transfer within 15 business days of receipt of the product.

Products showing signs of alteration by parties other than Virtue Star will not be eligible for buy-back. We reserve the right to adjust the product value based on quality inspection, providing justification for any changes.

Additional Notes:

  • A loss of the original product certificate will incur a deduction of Rs. 1500.
  • Customers must provide the original invoice and certificate for exchange or buyback transactions.
  • Products purchased from stores other than VirtueStars Stores are not eligible for exchange or buyback.
  • Buyback is not applicable to Baguettes, Tapered, Choki and diamonds made on demand.
  • Exchange and buyback services do not extend to Polki, Pearl, and Colored Stones.
  • Discounts applied during the original purchase on the metal or stone value will be adjusted from the current market price during buyback or exchange. Additionally, any e-Points utilized during the purchase will be deducted from the Lifetime Exchange value.
  • Buyback services are applicable to products purchased up to a value of Rs. 4,99,999/-.
  • For products purchased through the EMI system, an amount equivalent to the remaining unpaid EMI will be deducted, in addition to the aforementioned deductions. Shipping and Insurance:

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs. A deduction of Rs. 1,000/- or actual delivery charges (whichever is higher) will apply.
